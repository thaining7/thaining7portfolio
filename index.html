About Me

In your world you can create anything you desire. Almost everything is going to happen for you automatically - you don't have to spend any time working or worrying. Use what you see, don't plan it. You can create the world you want to see and be a part of. You have that power. Just think about these things in your mind - then bring them into your world.

Just use the old one inch brush. That's a crooked tree. We'll send him to Washington. You could sit here for weeks with your one hair brush trying to do that - or you could do it with one stroke with an almighty brush. We don't want to set these clouds on fire. Let's have a happy little tree in here.
